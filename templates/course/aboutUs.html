About Us

This comprehensive initiative involves the curation and development of free courses focused on job readiness and skill empowerment, alongside the creation and maintenance of an accessible website and app for user engagement. It emphasizes networking and collaboration efforts to establish connections with potential partners, NGOs, and marginalized women. Human capital is prioritized, requiring a skilled team for content creation, community management, and partnership development. The initiative underscores the importance of a reliable technology infrastructure, including servers, software, and platform maintenance, to ensure a user-friendly experience. Building collaborative relationships is a key aspect, with a focus on partnerships with NGOs, corporations, and educators for content creation and support.

CURRENTLY CONNECTED NGOs

The Bhai Ghanayya Ji Charitable Trust, operating as 'Unique Home' since May 17th, 1993, is dedicated to caring for abandoned children, especially baby girls found in distressing conditions. The aim is the moral, social, cultural, and economic uplift of these children, transcending distinctions of caste, creed, and religion. Bibi Prakaash Kaur, the driving force and lifetime managing trustee, passionately rehabilitates those shunned by society, particularly in a region known for female foeticide. Despite her advanced age, she mothers 60 abandoned girls, providing them with a hopeful future and access to education. The trust appeals for support from kind-hearted individuals to contribute to this mass social effort, ensuring these children are raised as enlightened and self-respecting citizens, reclaiming their due place in society.

Gursidak Foundation, a registered NGO under the Companies Act 2013 (License Number 122619), is committed to offering free tuition classes and financial support to students facing economic barriers in pursuing higher education. With a vision for a society where every child receives quality education, the foundation believes in education as both a means and an end to a better life, empowering individuals and fostering economic and social independence. Recognizing the transformative impact of education, Gursidak Foundation emphasizes the interconnectedness of child education with family well-being, healthcare, and skills development for sustainable social transformation, aspiring to create opportunities for success and awareness on societal issues.
